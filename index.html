About

The game is a 2D platformer game where the player controls a character.

This is the character you will be controling. It will send Wave during Punching and Upper-Punching to kill Enemies.
It can also Crush Enemies by Jumping on them.

First Enemy, it's a GroundEnemy, can be killed using the punching or the crushing stategies.
It have 35% of spawning. Gives you 2 points killed by punching and 4 points killed by crushing.
Note: It's a GrassMonster.

Second Enemy, it's also a GroundEnemy, can be killed using the punching or the crushing stategies.
It have 15% of spawning. Gives you 3 points killed by punching and 5 points killed by crushing.

Third Enemy, it's a FlyingEnemy, can be killed using the Upper-punching stategy.
Gives you 1 point killed by Upperpunching.
Note: It's a Cute Bee but it's deadly.

You start having 5 hearts and everytime you collide with Enemies you will take damage and lose a heart. Game end when you lose all your hearts.

Instructions

Use ←↑↓→ or WASD keys to move.
Use → or D to go right and ← or A to go left.
The key Space with → or D will make you go faster and It will give you a boost in your jump which will make you jump higher.
↓ and S have no effect on ground.
You can Jump by pressing ↑ or W
By pressing Enter you can punch Ground Enemies, you get 2 / 3 points, and by pressing Enter while jumping you can kill Flying Enemies, you get 1 point.
And if you are Mid-air you can press ↓ or S to crush Ground Enemies and get 4 / 5 points.
To Check Enemies' Hitbox, press H.
Players with higher ratio get a higher Rank. And the ratio in this game is equal to (kills/time).
If you take a hit, you will lose a heart and get dizzy for few seconds, but you will not lose another heart if you get hit while you are dizzy.
